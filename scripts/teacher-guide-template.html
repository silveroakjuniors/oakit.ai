10. Important Rules

Do's

  • Mark attendance before 10:00 AM
  • Complete plan before leaving school
  • Send child journey notes weekly
  • Use Oakie for teaching help
  • Change password from default
  • Check calendar for holidays

Don'ts

  • No homework on weekends/holidays
  • Never share login credentials
  • No future date attendance
  • No negative language in notes
  • Never skip marking completion
Your principal can see your attendance timing, plan completion rate, and teaching streak. Consistency is tracked and visible.
