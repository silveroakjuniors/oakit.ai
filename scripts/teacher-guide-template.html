4. Oakie AI Chat & Voice Input

Your AI teaching assistant. Ask anything about curriculum, classroom management, or teaching strategies.

Curriculum Help

Explain topics, suggest activities, provide resources

Classroom Management

Handle crying, misbehavior, shy students, energy levels

Activity Ideas

Games, songs, rhymes, art projects for any subject

Format & Beautify

Format homework and notes for parents

Example Questions

  • "What activities can I do for counting today?"
  • "How to handle a child who won't stop crying?"
  • "Give me a rhyme for the letter B"
  • "What should I do if a child finishes early?"

Using the Mic Button (Voice Input)

Instead of typing, tap the microphone icon to speak your question or note. This works in:

  • Oakie Chat - Ask questions by speaking naturally
  • Homework - Dictate homework text instead of typing
  • Child Journey - Speak your observation notes
  • Class Notes - Dictate subject notes quickly

How: Tap mic → Speak clearly → Text appears automatically → Edit if needed → Send

Best for: Long messages, quick notes between classes, when hands are busy

Limit: 5 questions before marking completion. Complete the day to unlock more.

7. Child Journey

Send personalized daily updates about each child to their parents. This is one of the most valued features by parents.

Individual Student Notes

1
Go to Help > Child JourneyOpens the journey entry screen
2
Select a student from the listOr write for the currently selected student
3
Write or dictate your noteUse mic button for voice input
4
Tap "Ask Oakie" to beautifyAI formats your note in a warm, parent-friendly tone
5
Choose entry typeDaily / Weekly / Highlight
6
Save Only or Save & SendSave Only = history only. Save & Send = notifies parents immediately

Generic Class Note

Write one note that applies to the whole class (e.g., "Had a fun art day!"):

  • The note gets saved for every student individually
  • Use "Save & Send All" to notify all parents at once
  • Great for class-wide activities, celebrations, or general updates

History Tab

View Past Entries

All entries grouped by date. Filter by student or date range.

Edit or Delete

Modify entries before sending. Delete if entered by mistake.

Send Unsent

Send previously saved (but unsent) entries to parents individually or all at once.

Entry Types

Daily (routine), Weekly (summary), Highlight (special achievement)

Report Readiness (Observations)

Under the Report Readiness tab, add structured observations for report cards:

  • Categories: Cognitive, Language, Social, Emotional, Motor Skills, Creativity, Participation, Peer, Behavior
  • System alerts you if students are missing observations
  • These observations feed into term-end report card generation
Parents love receiving daily updates. Even a short "Had a great day!" makes a difference. Aim for at least one note per student per week.

11. Important Rules

Do's

  • Mark attendance every day before 10:00 AM
  • Complete the day's plan before leaving school
  • Send at least one child journey note per week per student
  • Use Oakie for teaching help - it knows your curriculum
  • Change your password from the default
  • Check the calendar for upcoming holidays
  • Use the mic button for quick dictation
  • Add observations regularly for report cards

Don'ts

  • No homework on weekends or holidays
  • Never share your login credentials
  • No future date attendance marking
  • No negative language in child journey notes
  • Never skip marking plan completion
  • Do not ignore low-attendance alerts
  • Do not leave observations to the last week of term
Your principal can see your attendance timing, plan completion rate, teaching streak, and parent engagement stats. Consistency is tracked and visible.
